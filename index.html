Why Protect Marine Ecosystems?

Oceans cover over 70% of the planet, providing food, climate regulation, and rich biodiversity. They face threats from plastic pollution, overfishing, acidification, and habitat loss from coastal development.

What Properties Can Do

  • Replace single-use supplies with reusable or recyclable alternatives.
  • Create rain gardens and permeable surfaces to reduce runoff.
  • Educate residents through workshops and signage.

Action Checklist (for Properties)

Waste Management

Install clear recycling stations, provide resident training, and connect with local recyclers.

Water & Waste

Promote water-saving fixtures and explore small-scale wastewater treatment options or partnerships.

Green Public Spaces

Convert roofs and courtyards into rain gardens and vegetated buffers to boost biodiversity.
